JuJu's Second Breakfast

❄️ Cold Outside, Glow Within

The morning was sharp with frostbite promises—mist clinging to the hills, the air crisp as cold apples. But inside Cobblestone Cottage, warmth curled around everything like a shawl. The fire in the hearth crackled cheerfully, its scent a blend of old wood, honey, and stories.

🍯 The Honeydrizzle Queen Returns

At the high seat near the table's edge sat JuJu the Pixie—small of stature, vast of spirit. Her eyes sparkled with that mischievous morning glint only reserved for second breakfasts.

Before her lay a plate fit for a forest queen:

  • Sunfruit rounds (a secret pancake recipe only Mama knew)
  • Cloudmilk cream (soft and tart and perfect)
  • A generous drizzle of golden honey that glistened like fairy-thread

Pop Pop lifted her into place and bowed dramatically.

“The Honeydrizzle Queen has returned!”

JuJu giggled and took her first bite with royal reverence. Mama, the quiet keeper of the skillet, watched with a smile soft as butter melting on warm bread.

🐉 Buttercup the Brave, Stationed at the Gate

Just a few steps away, on the other side of the hallway gate, sat Buttercup the Brave—a baby dragon of curly gold fuzz and flopsy ears.

She had not been exiled. She had been stationed—a noble post to prevent her from leaping joyfully all over JuJu mid-breakfast, which she was very good at.

Buttercup's snout pressed through the bars, her green eyes fixed on every spoonful with the solemn intensity of a guard at a royal feast.

“After we visit Omberry, I'll give you double snuggles. Promise.”

Buttercup blinked once, slowly. Her sacred vow of waiting was sealed.

🧣 Off to Omberry's in Lonchester

As the sun rose higher, the morning's adventure called.

  • Maximillion, JuJu's radiant mother, was packing bags for the journey
  • Baby OonaBaboona was already bundled in her berrywrap
  • Jophes, JuJu's gentle dad, kissed her crown before heading off to help those with soft voices and strong hearts
  • And far across the hills in Lonchester, the bustling city, Omberry—a flour-dusted grandmother full of warmth and cinnamon—was baking for their arrival

🔥 And Still, the Cottage Held Them

JuJu's plate was nearly clean. A ribbon of honey on her chin. Buttercup waited. The fire still danced. Mama wiped JuJu's face with a warm cloth. Pop Pop poured another coffee and said nothing at all.

Outside, the wind was cold.
But inside Cobblestone Cottage, something ancient and golden stirred—
A memory in the making.
A morning never to be exactly repeated.
A little myth born of honey, fire, and love.

“The hearth holds more than warmth—it holds the tale.”
